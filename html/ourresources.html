​Young People's Skills and Capacity Assessment Report

This assessment provides a brief overview of young people’s capacity and skills in civic engagement in Lumbini province, Nepal. It outlines key findings based on a week long field visits and interaction with 30 youths and civil society leaders from the province. This report examines the gaps and challenges young people are facing to strengthen their activism and movements and further provides a strategy to ‘Freedom Studio’ for running Artivism Media Fellowship’, supported by The Asia Foundation and USAID Civil Society and Media Activity.

​Strategy Development Training - Workbook

The strategy development training workbook consists of training guidelines and resources that takes ‘Artivism Fellows’ into an intensive strategy development process for the activism and movement they lead. The workbook consists of manual and guidance on problem tree and solution tree analysis, developing common visions and values, situation analysis and stakeholders mapping, developing strategic goals and objectives, result chain mapping and lastly developing a fellowship plan. Such sessions helps fellows to dig deep down into the problem of their community and develop possible intervention strategy.

​Creative and Technical Skill Development Training - Workbook

The creative and technical skill development training workbook consists of training guidelines and resources that equip ‘Active Fellows’ with a skills that are necessary for digital activism. The workbook consists of explanations and practical session guide on mobile photography, videography, audio, SFX, scriptwriting, storyboarding, graphic design etc. Workbook will also take participants through ‘Pre-Production’, ‘Production’, and ‘Post Production’ process, through which fellows enhance their theoretical and practical skills on camera handling, editing and publication of creative artivism contents.
