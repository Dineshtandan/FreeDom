LGBTIQ + Rights

Freedom Studio proudly supports the LGBTQI movement in Nepal, championing the rights and dignity of individuals across the spectrum of gender and sexual identities. Our commitment is to foster an inclusive society where everyone can live freely without fear of stigmas, discrimination or violence through optimizing their rights to creative expression. Under our initiatives, we amplify the voices of LGBTQI individuals, document their struggles and triumphs, and provide platforms for their stories to be heard of struggles and successes. We co-create with grassroots LGBTQI-led organizations and activists to advocate for policy and legal reforms, social acceptance, and equal rights. Our support includes creative advocacy campaigns, artivism workshops, and community events aimed at raising awareness and educating the public about LGBTQI issues. We also offer strategic, creative and technical support to LGBTQI activists and organizations, helping them to effectively lead their campaigns and navigate the challenges they face. Freedom Studio's support extends to creating safe spaces for dialogue and expression, ensuring that LGBTQI individuals have the resources and support they need to thrive through strategic, technical and creative support to their own led missions.
