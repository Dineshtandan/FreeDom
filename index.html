Where Art meets Activism
Freedom Studio is a pro-democracy, movement building initiative that strategically and creatively supports civic mobilization. We invest in building leadership and capacities of marginalized citizens especially younger ones, in expanding full potential of the social activism/movement they are leading. We mainly train them on leadership approaches, strategic development, nonviolent resistance, artivism, and digital media campaign. We believe such efforts will lift their power status in the society and challenges unjust social norms and practices.
INITIATIVES
Movement Resource Center
Movement Resource Center (MRC) is a dynamic platform for activists and leaders of social movements to connect and innovatively respond to injustices and civic restrictions.
The Artivism Lab
​The Artivism Lab is a creative space that assesses and empowers artists and activists on using diverse forms of art in social and political issues.​
Center for Civic Media and Innovation Hub
Movement Media uses new media and technologies to design, create and foster civic engagement and ethical flow of information.
Artvocacy Academy
Artvocacy Academy, a pioneering initiative by Freedom Studio, invented to harness the power of art as a dynamic tool for advocacy and legal activism.
